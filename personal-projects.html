Learning Outside of Class

Clubs and Campus Activities

I stay involved with SLCC’s student community through two clubs that fuel my academic and career passions:

  • Utah Criminology Student Association – Diving deep into justice systems, law enforcement topics, and networking with fellow future forensic experts.
  • SLCC Student Nurse Organization – Because being on track for an RN isn’t enough. I like being involved with real-world prep, resource sharing, and supporting my fellow nursing students.

Hobbies & Talents

Outside of classes, I’ve done the most — and it shows.

  • Healthcare & Nursing: CNA certified, dialysis tech experience, pediatric care, senior care — I stay booked and bedside.
  • Gaming & Tech: Competitive Xbox grinder, obsessed with optimizing systems and frame rates. My hobby taught me teamwork, patience, and how to set up an Ethernet cable without rage-quitting.
  • Manual Driving: I drive stick. That’s it. Jeep Wranglers, hills, and all. No stall, all skill.
  • Creative Writing: My humor and storytelling got me through trauma and straight into academic excellence. I don’t just write for grades — I write to survive (and maybe to go viral).
  • Cultural Roots: Raised in a Mexican household, bilingual with pride. I carry my family’s story with me everywhere — in every paper, every goal, every meal, and every “sí se puede.”
  • CLEP Exams (for fun?!): I took CLEP tests I didn’t even need because they were paid for. Academic curiosity activated.

Work Experience

I currently work as a healthcare professional, with hands-on experience in dialysis, pediatrics, and senior care. I also work in real estate and property management, helping clients while managing my own schedule. Flexibility, compassion, and multitasking? Yeah, I got those.

Travel & Volunteering

  • Travel: I’ve visited Mexico to connect with family and roots, and I’ve traveled across the U.S. Gaining cultural perspective helps me grow — and helps my future patients feel seen.
  • Volunteer Work: I’ve helped in medical spaces, mentored students, and supported peers in both real estate and nursing. Mentoring and community engagement keep me grounded while I chase my dreams.

Services I’ve Provided

  • Mentorship: For both new real estate agents and aspiring healthcare students.
  • Tutoring: Nursing and real estate coursework support — I got you.
  • Community Involvement: Volunteering in healthcare, participating in charity events, and giving back to the communities that raised me.
