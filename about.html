My Goals

My personal and academic goals while at SLCC

Short-Term Goals

  • Practice Self-Compassion and Build Confidence: ‘As I navigate through the challenges... learning from mistakes is part of the process.’
  • Reach Out to Professors During Office Hours: I will make an effort to connect... help me succeed in my courses.
  • Form Study Groups with Classmates: I will find a study buddy... gain different perspectives.
  • Develop Consistent Study Habits: I will establish a regular routine... plan ahead for exams.
  • Take Care of My Mental Health: I will focus on self-care, relaxation, and stress management.

Long-Term Goals

  • Graduate with Dual Degrees: Criminal Justice from SLCC and Nursing from Joyce University.
  • Secure a Specialized Career: Forensic nursing or healthcare law enforcement.
  • Achieve Financial Stability: Support myself and give back to my family.
