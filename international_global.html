International & Global (IG)

POLS 2100 – International Politics

This course pulled no punches. It asked us to break down the motives behind world conflict, diplomacy, and global decision-making—and gave me the perfect excuse to roast U.S. foreign policy with citations. My final project? A theory breakdown of the U.S. invasion and withdrawal from Afghanistan through the lens of IR theory.

Signature Assignment: IR Theory & Afghanistan

This embedded paper explores the U.S. occupation of Afghanistan (2001–2021) through Realism and Liberalism—aka Power and PR. Strap in.

Reflection

Writing this paper gave me the perfect opportunity to look at international politics and say: “Y’all really thought this was subtle?” The assignment asked me to choose the best international relations theory or theories to explain why the U.S. invaded, stayed, and then dipped out of Afghanistan between 2001 and 2021. So I got to work, diving into the mess that is global policy, war decisions, and diplomatic branding...
